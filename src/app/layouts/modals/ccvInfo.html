The CVV Number ("Card Verification Value") on your credit card or debit card is a 3 digit number on VISA®, MasterCard® and Discover® branded credit and debit cards. On your American Express® branded credit or debit card it is a 4 digit numeric code.

It is found on the signature strip on the back of the card for VISA®, MasterCard® and Discover® branded credit and debit cards. and the 4 digits above the card number on the front of American Express® branded credit or debit card.
